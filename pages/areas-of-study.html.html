About our RTO

Australian Training Company established an RTO in 1998 in response to increasing demands for tailored education programs. TC's training operation promotes flexible study arrangements and an inclusive learning culture.

Government Funding

ATC's RTO operates state funded training in the following states: NSW - NSW Smart and Skilled ACT - Skilled Capital QLD - User Choice

Industry Accreditation

Australian Training Company is a nationally Registered Training Organisation (RTO), approved by the Australian Skills Quality Authority (ASQA) RTO#5673. Our programs meet the strict guidelines set out by State and Federal Governments and their training bodies.

Our trainers are qualified professionals in their field of expertise, having studied and worked for a number of years to gain skills and experience as well as being trained trainers who undertake ongoing professional development to maintain currency and industry standards.
