Structure of Training

Explore our diverse training programs designed to provide you with the skills and knowledge needed for success in your chosen field.

Our student learnbook makes learning even easier, where students can complete their studies anywhere, anytime and on multiple devices.

Backed by Moodle and E-Creators, students will have around the clock access to their learning platform. Here they can access all learning materials, supporting videos and still receive the same level of dedicated trainer support just like in the classroom.

How will E-learning benefit me as a student?

Online learning may not be for everyone, but here are a few reasons why it may be ideal for you.

Flexible & convenient: You can complete your studies anywhere, at any time and on multiple devices. Perfect for fitting your studies in around existing commitments.

Specialised trainer support: Direct contact with a dedicated trainer will be on-hand to help support, motivate and keep you on track. You may contact your trainer via phone, email, or arrange a teleconference session.

Learning is individual: Studying in large groups isn't for everyone. Studying online removes the 'peer pressure' of classroom study.
