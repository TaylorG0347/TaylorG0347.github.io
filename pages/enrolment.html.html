Discover the steps to enrol in our programs and the requirements to kickstart your educational journey with ATC.

Our Registered Training Organisation (RTO ID: 5673) is able to offer a range of qualifications that are recognised and valued by employers Australia wide. You are provided with the best opportunity to learn no matter where you work or live through flexible education programs.

All assessors are fully qualified and have extensive current industry experience. They provide regular support, guidance and mentoring to ensure you succeed.

Flexible options include:

- Enrolments can be considered year round

- Relevant and up to date learning practices

- Recognition of Prior Learning Pathways

- Tailored programs to meet your needs

Enrolment Process

Follow these steps to successfully enrol in our programs:

  1. Explore our areas of study and training programs to find the right fit for your goals.
  2. Review the specific entry requirements for your chosen program.
  3. Complete the online application form with accurate information.
  4. Submit any required documents, such as proof of previous qualifications and identification.
  5. Wait for confirmation from our admissions team. You may be contacted for additional details or interviews.
  6. Once accepted, follow the provided instructions for payment and course commencement.

Enrolment Requirements

Before applying, ensure you meet the general enrolment requirements:

  • Minimum age requirement: 18 years
  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • English language proficiency (for non-native speakers)
